Chikkin's TFT Cheat Sheet

6 Cultists

Early: Elise, Twisted Fate, Evelynn
Mid: Kalista, Jhin, Kennen, Riven, Cassopeia
Late: Zilean, Azir

6 Cultists Tips

Stack cultists early and replace reds as you go
Chosen Cultist 100% needed
Protect Jhin

Shade Spirits

Early: Zed, Teemo, Yuumi, Kindred
Mid: Evelynn, Cassopeia, Ahri
Late: Kayn

Shade Spirits Tips

Level up early to gain winstreak until 6
Slow roll to 7 and find Zed 3
Easy clap

6 Enlightened

Early: Nami, Janna, Pyke
Mid: Morgana, Talon, Irelia, Shen
Late: Ezreal

6 Enlightened Tips

Weak early game, try to get strong champs
Ensure you get Talon's Items first
Blue buff on Janna is good

Moon Spirits

Early: Lissandra, Diana, Aphelios, Teemo
Mid: Yuumi, Kindred, Shen, Ahri
Late: Roll Moonlights to level 3

Moon Spirits Tips

Do not level up early and save gold
Slow roll at 5 to get Liss/Diana
Slow roll further to gold Aphelios

6 Elderwood

Early: Maokai, Lulu, Hecarim
Mid: Nunu, Veigar, Sejuani, Ashe, Ahri
Late: Roll champs to 3 or save and hit 9

6 Elderwood Tips

Get Brawler/Elder synergy early
Quicksilver on Ashe is decent
You can replace Hec with Sett

Brawler Ashe

Early: Maokai, Sylas, Vi
Mid: Nunu, Warwick, Ashe
Late: Sett, Ezreal

Brawler Ashe Tips

Only play if you have a spatula+cloak

Vanguard/Mystic Ahri

Early: Garen, Nidalee, Vayne, Thresh
Mid: Yuumi, Sejuani, Aatrox, Cass, Shen
Late: Ahri, Zilean

Vanguard/Mystic Ahri Tips

Put items into Nid and replace as you go
Start with Vanguard/Sharpshooters
Pivot to Ahri when she's level 2

6 Mages

Early: Annie, Nami, Janna, Twisted Fate, Lulu
Mid: Veigar, Yuumi
Late: Ahri

6 Mages Tips

Chosen Elder, if chosen Mage, drop TF for Elder
